# What is the ADA, exactly?

The Americans with Disabilities Act is an expansive piece of legislation that ensures that people with disabilities have equal access to basic things like transportation, businesses, and local government resources.

## How does it apply to Cuyana as a business?

In plenty of ways! Our stores have requirements around wheelchair accessibility among other legal requirements, but today we're discussing ADA compliance around Interactive Accessibility.
